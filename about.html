A Little About Myself

My name is Muhammad Firdaus Ilmi Bin Suzarwadi. I am 22 years old and in semester 5 in Bachelor of Information Science (Hons) Records Management (IM246) student at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Puncak Perdana. I was born on 5th February 2000 at HUKM Hospital Cheras, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. I have three siblings and I am the oldest child in the family.


I want to tell you a little about my family. My family is quite large. My family consists of my dad, mum, one sister, one brother and myself. Dad and mom are the individuals I love and respect the most in my life. My mum is a teacher and my dad is a Polis Bantuan at UiTM Machang. My mother is 45 years old and my father is 50 years old. They have been dedicated their lives in raising the 3 of us and ensure that we become successful individuals in this world and hereafter. I thank God to have such great parents. My sister is 19 years old and my brother is 16 years old and both of them are still studying. I am very thankful to God because my life is complete with the presence of them all.

By playing online games, I can add friends from all over the world because online games cover the whole world. Online games have been my hobby since I was little. However, I never neglected my learning sessions. I will divide the time to study and also the time to play. If there is an assignment that I need to complete, I make sure to complete it first before playing online games.
